Aikido

Aikido is a modern way of self defense, created by Morihei Ueshiba.
Aikido's goal was to create an art that practitioners could use to defend themselfes, while also protecting from injury (by the protector).
The main focuss of Aikido on techniques, without using any (extra) strength. Quite on the contrary.
When using excess strength to make up for any mistake in the technique, the technique will simply fail altogether!

Playing Keyboard

Although I do frequently play the keyboard of my computer, I also grew up in quite a musical family.
This most likely helped me start playing an instrument, with my preference to the same instrument my dad plays: The keyboard.

Besides lessons, I also used to participate in an anual event at the end of the year, called "Rock & Loll".
It consisting of a bunch of musicians with a love for their instrument coming together, along with a list of songs they want to play around a specific theme, and putting together a small band for each song that ultimately gets played after a selection.

I've participated 3 times, and really miss being unable to participate due to me living on my own in a different city as of writing.
